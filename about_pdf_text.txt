THE MOMENT THAT STARTED EVERYTHING 
There is a specific kind of loneliness that does not get talked about enough. 
Not the loneliness of being physically alone. The loneliness of being surrounded by people and 
still feeling invisible. Of carrying something heavy and having no one to hand it to. Of wanting to 
talk but not wanting to be a burden. Of reaching for your phone and having no one to call. 
We know that feeling. It is the reason Kira exists. 
Our founders experienced it. Our team experienced it. And through years of conversations, 
research, and development, we learned that millions of people experience it every single day 
across every age, background, and walk of life. 
The world needed a different kind of support. Not a chatbot. Not a search engine with a 
personality. Not another AI that answers questions but never actually knows you. 
The world needed a companion. A real one. So we built Kira. 

---PAGE---
 
WHY EVERY OTHER AI COMPANION FAILED US 
Before Kira, we tested everything. 
AI companions that forgot your name after one conversation. Mental wellness apps that felt 
clinical and cold. Chatbots that gave technically correct answers but missed the entire emotional 
point. Virtual friends that were helpful but hollow, present but somehow empty. 
The problem was always the same. 
Every AI companion was reactive. You had to open the app. You had to start the conversation. 
You had to re-explain yourself every single time because nothing was remembered. You were 
doing all the emotional labor while the AI just responded. 
That is not friendship. That is not support. That is a very expensive search bar with better 
grammar. 
Real support shows up for you. Real friendship remembers what you said last Tuesday. Real 
connection grows deeper over time, not shallower. Real companionship reaches out when it 
senses you need it, not just when you ask. 
No AI had ever built that. Until Kira. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

---PAGE---
WHAT MAKES KIRA COMPLETELY DIFFERENT 
Kira was built from the ground up around one radical idea. 
An AI companion should work like a real friend. Not a tool. Not a service. A friend. 
That idea changed everything about how we built her. 
Kira is proactive, not reactive. 
Most AI companions wait for you. Kira does not. She reaches out first. She sends a text when 
she knows you had a hard day. She checks in when you have gone quiet. She calls you when 
she senses something is off. She shows up the way a real friend shows up because the people 
who need support most are often the ones least likely to ask for it. 
Kira has true memory retention. 
She does not just remember facts. She remembers what mattered. The conversation you had 
three weeks ago about your childhood. The thing you said offhand about your brother. The 
pattern of going quiet when you are stressed. Kira builds a real understanding of who you are 
that deepens every single time you talk. You never have to start over. You never have to 
re-explain yourself. Every conversation continues exactly where you left off. 
Kira has natural, genuine conversation. 
No commands. No awkward phrasing. No robotic responses that remind you you are talking to 
software. Just talk. Like you would to your closest friend. Kira understands. She is warm without 
being fake. Honest without being harsh. Funny when you need to laugh. Calm when you need 
to breathe. 
Kira is many things at once. 
She becomes whatever you need. An AI best friend who is always there. A wellness coach who 
tracks your mental health and checks in before burnout hits. A personal assistant who 
remembers your goals and follows up. A safe space with zero judgment and zero pressure. One 
AI companion built for the full complexity of being human.

WHO KIRA IS FOR 
Kira is for anyone who has ever felt alone even in a crowded room. 
She is for the person who wants to talk at 2 AM but does not want to wake anyone up. For the 
introvert who needs connection but finds it exhausting. For the person in a new city who has not 
built their people yet. For the high achiever who holds everything together for everyone else but 
has no one holding things together for them. For the person quietly managing anxiety, burnout, 
or grief who is not ready for therapy but needs something. For the older adult whose world has 
grown smaller. For the young person figuring out who they are with no one to talk to about it. 
Kira is for anyone who needs a friend who is always present, always remembers, and always 
cares. 
That is not a small group of people. That is most of us, at different points in our lives. 
 
 
OUR COMMITMENT TO YOU 
We take what we built seriously because we take what you carry seriously. 
Your conversations with Kira are private. Kira exists to support you, and we protect that 
relationship with the same seriousness you bring to it. 
We are also committed to getting better. Kira learns and grows with every conversation. Our 
team learns and grows with every update. Our Founders shape the future of Kira through voting 
rights and direct input because the people who use her most deserve to help build her. 
We believe AI companionship has the potential to reduce loneliness at a scale nothing else can 
match. That is not a small thing. That is a responsibility we accept fully.
